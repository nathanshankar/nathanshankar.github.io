About me

I'm delighted to welcome you to my website, where I have the pleasure of sharing my story as a passionate enthusiast in the field of Robotics. My name is Nathan Shankar, and I have dedicated 4 years of my life to mastering the intricacies of Electrical Engineering. What initially sparked my fascination was watching several sequels of Transformers, and since then, my passion has only grown stronger, driving me to excel in everything I do.

Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to work on a diverse range of projects and tackle complex challenges within Electric Vehicles. By specializing in BMS, I have consistently delivered exceptional results, earning the trust and satisfaction of my professors and collaborators from other universities.

What truly sets me apart is my insatiable thirst for knowledge and innovation. I am deeply committed to staying at the forefront of industry trends and advancements, constantly seeking ways to expand my expertise and explore new possibilities. This dedication enables me to offer my clients cutting-edge solutions that are tailored to their unique needs, ensuring their success in an ever-evolving landscape.

In addition to my technical proficiency, I place great importance on open and effective communication. I thrive in collaborative environments and have had the privilege of working closely with a wide range of people in various project teams. By actively listening and understanding their visions, I am able to translate ideas into reality, fostering strong relationships and achieving remarkable outcomes.

I invite you to explore my portfolio and reach out to me. Together, let's embark on a transformative journey, bringing your ideas to life and achieving extraordinary results. I eagerly look forward to connecting with you and creating something remarkable together.
